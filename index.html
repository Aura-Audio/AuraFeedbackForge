Feature Guide

  • Zero-Dropout Routing: Add/remove frequencies while playing without audio stutters.
  • Saturation: Adds analog-style harmonic distortion to the feedback path.
  • Stereo Spatialization: Frequencies are automatically panned across the stereo field based on pitch.
  • Native LFO: Volume modulation is now hardware-accelerated via Web Audio for zero-latency drift.
  • Background Play: Audio continues when switching tabs or locking your screen.

Note: Audio feedback is actively restricted by built-in limiters and a delay node to protect your hearing and prevent context crashes.
