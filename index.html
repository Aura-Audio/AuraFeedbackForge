Auto Rate Modulation

  • Sequence mode: Steps through 0.1 → 1 → 5 → 10 → 15 → 20 → 25 → 50 → 100 → 1000 Hz in order, then loops. 0.1 Hz is a 10-second breathing cycle. 1000 Hz (0.001s) is deep ring modulation — the modulator becomes a audible tone creating metallic sidebands.
  • Random mode: Shuffles the 10 rates into a new random order each loop. Every rate plays once per loop, but the sequence is unpredictable. The step indicators highlight wherever the jump lands.
  • Dwell Time: 0.3s to 60s per step. At 60s dwell the full sequence takes 10 minutes. All transitions are pre-scheduled on the audio thread for sample-accurate timing.
  • Waveform: Sine/square/triangle/sawtooth selection applies to both sequence and random modes.
